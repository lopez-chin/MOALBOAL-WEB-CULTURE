Traditional Cuisine

Moalboal Cuisine

Moalboal is very known when it comes to seafoods.Moalboal’s cuisine is a delightful blend of traditional Filipino flavors and the unique culinary influences of Cebu. Known for its fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and hearty dishes, Moalboal offers visitors an unforgettable gastronomic experience.
Seafood takes center stage in Moalboal's culinary scene, thanks to its coastal location. Freshly caught fish, crabs, squid, and prawns are often grilled, steamed, or incorporated into local delicacies like kinilaw, a dish of raw fish marinated in vinegar, calamansi juice, and spices. These dishes reflect the region’s reliance on the bounties of the sea.Moalboal's cuisine embodies the town’s culture and hospitality, offering visitors a chance to savor authentic Filipino dishes while enjoying the warm, tropical ambiance of this charming coastal destination.
